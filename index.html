About me

Building a quiet life, on purpose.

I'm Léa, a Montréaler by choice, born in France of Senegalese parents. I work in digital marketing by day and spend my free time somewhere between a café window and my camera viewfinder.

My early twenties were harder than I let on back then. Moving countries after graduation, working two part-time jobs while finishing my degree, learning what it meant to build stability from scratch. That period shaped me in ways I'm still unpacking. What I know now is that I don't take the calm for granted.
